## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information in connection with your use of our Services:

**Identifiers:** Name, email address (used only for authentication and identification).

**Usage Data:** Applications in use, browser URLs, and related activity as captured by the License Logic agent or browser extension.

**System Data:** Device identifiers, device name, operating system version, and process-level activity (excluding sensitive system/security processes).

**Directory Data (via Identity Provider Integrations):** When you connect Microsoft Entra ID or Google Workspace, we access:
- Employee directory information (name, email, job title, department)
- Device registry (device name, OS, compliance status)
- Application sign-in logs 

This access is **read-only**. We do not modify, delete, or write any data to your identity provider.

**Operational Data:** Log files generated by our service for security, performance, and troubleshooting.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Email content or attachments
- Keystrokes or screen captures
- File contents or documents
- Passwords or payment information
- Audio, video, or webcam data

## Data Governance and Security

**Where Data Goes**: All customer data collected by our agents and integrations is encrypted and transmitted securely to Amazon Web Services (AWS), located in the United State.s

**Identity Provider Tokens:** OAuth access and refresh tokens from Entra ID and Google Workspace are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage.

**How It's Protected:** Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 with RSA key management). Access is restricted with role-based access controls, MFA, and audit logging.

**How Long we Keep It:**
- Operational log data: 7 days (auto-deleted)
- Usage data: 12 months (configurable by customer)
- Account data: Duration of contract + 30 days
- Aggregated analytics: May be retained longer for reporting

**Data Deletion:** Customer data is deleted within 30 days of contract termination. On-demand deletion available upon written request.
